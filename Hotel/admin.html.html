💼 How to Sell NovaServe to Cafés
Your complete guide to acquiring restaurant clients and growing NovaFlux.
🎯
Who to Target
Focus on small-to-medium cafés, restaurants, dhabas, and cloud kitchens that have 5–30 tables. They can't afford custom POS software but desperately need to cut waiter costs and speed up service. Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore are goldmines — thousands of such places.
💬
Your Pitch (30 seconds)
"Sir, your customers scan a QR code at the table, browse your entire menu, place their order — and it appears directly on your screen. No app download, no hardware, no waiter needed for order-taking. Your staff focuses on cooking and serving. You increase table turnover by 30%."
😱
Pain Points to Mention
• Waiter takes wrong order → kitchen waste
• Customer waits 10 min to order → leaves
• Can't update menu prices daily
• No idea which dishes are popular
• Peak hours are chaotic — orders pile up
• Paper menus get dirty and outdated
✨
What You Offer
• QR Menu — works on any phone, no app
• Live order tracking — kitchen sees instantly
• Special Occasion Mode — birthday surprises
• Customer feedback — built-in ratings
• Analytics — peak hours, top dishes
• WhatsApp integration (upsell)
• Setup in under 1 hour
🪜 Step-by-Step Sales Process
1
Visit & Demo On-Site (Week 1)
Walk into a café during off-peak hours (2–5pm). Ask for the owner, not manager. Show the customer app on your phone. Say "Your customers order like this." Let them feel it. Takes 5 minutes.
2
Offer a Free Trial (Days 1–7)
Set up their menu in the admin panel right there. Takes 20 minutes. Generate QR codes for 2 tables. Let them try it free for 7 days. When their customers love it, closing is easy.
3
Show the Results (Day 7)
Return after 7 days. Show the analytics — orders placed, peak hours, top dishes. Ask "Did your service feel smoother?" They'll say yes. Then present pricing.
4
Close with Urgency
Say "I'm onboarding only 3 more cafés in [area] this month. After that, pricing goes up." Offer a 3-month or annual plan with a small discount for upfront payment.
5
Referrals = Free Growth
After a successful client, ask "Do you know 2 café owners who'd benefit from this?" Happy clients are your best salespeople. Offer them 1 month free for each referral that converts.

🎨 How Cafés Add Their Menu & Change Theme
📋
Adding the Menu
Go to Menu → Add Dish. Enter dish name, price, description, photo URL (from their Google Maps listing or a photo you take). Mark veg/non-veg, popular, chef's pick. Takes 2 minutes per dish. A 30-item menu = ~1 hour setup.
🎨
Changing the Theme
In the customer HTML file, change the CSS variables at the top: --caramel, --espresso, --cream. Change the café name in the hero section. For custom logos, replace the SVG. As a service, you can charge ₹2,000 one-time for custom branding.
